# ⛏️ Survival Features

Chertia Requiem features a number of fun and quality-of-life changes to the vanilla experience.

### Economy

Chertia Requiem uses a custom economy system which runs on a currency called **crowns**. Crowns are typically earned through:

* Selling fish to a Fishmonger.
* Selling ores to an Ore Vendor.
* Participating in story events.
* _And more, coming soon..._

Crowns can be exchanged for a variety of things. There are a number of vendors on the spawn islands, including a Rare Block Vendor and an Alchemist, who generates randomized potions. More ways to earn and spend crowns are always being added!

### Custom Items

There are quite a few custom items with textures provided by a server-side resource pack. Many additional food items have been added, as well as several 'key' items and tools with special functionality. For example, you can craft a Builder's Hook which will extend your reach when placed in your offhand; Repair Oil will partially repair every item in your inventory; Portal Stones allow you to quickly teleport mobs from one place to another; and many more!

Instruments can be crafted or purchased from the Instrument Vendor. The Starlight Bell, crafted using a special item, allows for fast-travel between certain locations.

### Fishing

We have a custom fishing system. There are over 25 types of fish, and each fish you catch will carry a set of stats that determine its 'worth' in crowns. These fish can be sold to a Fishmonger.

### Quarry

Several 'quarries' exist around the world and operate similar to 'Prison' servers. You can spend all the time you'd like mining the available resources, selling the ores you collect. You are required to regenerate the quarry, which costs crowns, before you leave.

### And more...

There are so many more custom-coded features on Chertia Requiem. For example...

* Right click on a stair or slab to sit down.
* Custom sleep-voting system where one player can anonymously cancel skipping the night.
* Right click to open shulker boxes in your hand.
* Happy Ghasts like certain items and blocks; use a Slimeball to stop their AI, use an ice block to shrink them down, use a magma block to make them grow again, and use sugar to temporarily speed them up!
* Tools & weapons will not be dropped when you die.
* Craft Bandages & Aid Kits to heal other players.
* Throw bricks!
* Step on a froglight to be sent flying. Verdant froglights can be used to teleport.
* The Ender Dragon will always drop an Elytra & a Dragon Head.
* Sneak to remove any arrows stuck to you.
