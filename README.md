# Chertia

### 📖 **Chertia**

Chertia is a Roleplay survival multiplayer server (SMP) focused on gods, magic, and monsters. On the server, every one of your actions is also the action of your character. Want to stir up chaos with a mischievous character? Dream of running a cozy inn, offering refuge to weary travelers? Or perhaps you see yourself as a wandering nomad, exploring every corner of the world? The possibilities are endless.

### 📖 Getting Started

Currently, we're not running a season of Chertia. However, you can join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/VSPpYwW/) to find out what we're up to next. Once you're in, send a message in our [#join-here](https://discord.com/channels/555458092486230058/1473359125881684188) channel with the information requested.&#x20;
